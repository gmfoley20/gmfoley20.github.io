Louisiana State University

About LSU

Louisiana State University (often referred to as LSU) is a public research university in Baton Rouge, Louisiana. Founded in 1853, it is the flagship institution of the Louisiana State University System.

Quick Facts:

  • Established: 1853
  • Location: Baton Rouge, Louisiana
  • Mascot: Mike the Tiger
  • Colors: Purple and Gold

Academics

Louisiana State University is a leading research-intensive institution and offers over 200 programs of study. The university is known for its strong programs in areas such as engineering, business, the arts, and more.

Athletics

The LSU Tigers, the university's athletic teams, compete in the NCAA Division I and are members of the Southeastern Conference. The school's football team has a rich history and has claimed multiple national championships.
