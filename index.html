About FoleyGPT at LSU

FoleyGPT is a leading program within Louisiana State University. Here, we're dedicated to advancing knowledge in generative AI programming and machine learning. With a main campus of more than 2,000 acres and over 45,000 students, we bring in the best and brightest.

History

Founded in 1860, LSU is a testament to the ambitions of the people of Louisiana. Throughout its history, LSU has served the state, the nation, and the world through extensive, multipurpose programs encompassing instruction, research, and public service.

Research

LSU is among the top-tier research universities in the country. FoleyGPT, among other programs, plays a key role in advancing cutting-edge AI research.

Campus Life

With over 250 student organizations, LSU provides numerous opportunities for students to enhance their academic, social, cultural, and personal development.
